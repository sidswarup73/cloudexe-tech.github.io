Why Cloudexe

Traditional clouds weren't built for research.

They were built for IT teams. Here's what that costs you.

1
Cost vs. availability tradeoff
Reserved instances are expensive. Spot instances disappear. Research teams end up either overpaying or waiting in queues when they need GPUs most.
2
Requires a sysadmin to operate
Hyperscaler UX is designed for IT professionals. Researchers end up managing VMs, containers, and networking instead of running experiments.
3
Constant syncing and rebuilding
Every new session means re-uploading code, reinstalling dependencies, and waiting for environments to spin up. Context-switching kills research momentum.
How it works

Persistent environment. On-demand GPUs.

Cloudexe separates your environment from your compute — solving all three problems at once.

1
JIT GPU architecture
GPUs attach to your job on demand and release when done. The shared fleet stays highly utilized — availability stays high, your bill stays low.
2
Persistent base instance. No rebuilds, ever.
Each researcher gets a personal environment that's always on. Your code, tools, and dependencies are exactly where you left them.
3
Simple lab management. No IT team needed.
Onboard users, set quotas, manage projects — all from one clean dashboard. Cloudexe handles the orchestration so your team can focus on research.
