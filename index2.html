Why DevCloud

Traditional clouds weren't built for research.

They were built for IT teams. DevCloud is built for the people actually running the experiments.

Zero setup time
Your environment is always ready. No bootstrapping, no waiting.
No cost vs. availability tradeoff
JIT GPU allocation keeps costs down without sacrificing access.
No cloud complexity
No pods, no VMs to juggle. Built for researchers, not sysadmins.
What is DevCloud

One platform for development, training, and scaling.

GPU supply, a persistent environment, and simple team management — combined into one tool.

Stay in flow

No syncing. No rebuilding. Your environment persists exactly as you left it.

Simple lab management

Users, quotas, projects — handled. Dashboards and usage tracking built in.

Always-available GPUs

Run when you need to. Global neocloud fleet keeps availability high at all times.
